# Furniture Showroom Catalog Template

A calm, catalog-first showroom environment designed for premium physical products. Optimized for visual breathing room and high-fidelity product exploration.

## Value Proposition
- Translating physical elegance into a quiet, structured digital space.
- Showroom-focused, collection-first design that moves beyond standard e-commerce.
- Tailored for high-end furniture showrooms, artisanal physical products, and bespoke interior design hubs.

## Pages Included
1. **Home**: High-end hero showcase, featured collection previews, category quick filters, interior design trust block, and localized visit CTAs.
2. **Collections**: Visual grouping of furniture families with designer attributions and direct catalog link support.
3. **Products / Catalog**: Premium product listing with complete search filters, price tags, and low-friction localized product inquiry support.
4. **Projects / Inspiration**: Visual gallery of curated rooms/projects and 3D consultation scheduling prompts.
5. **About / Showroom**: Heritage story, craftsmanship philosophy, and localized trust pillars.
6. **Contact / Visit Showroom**: Appointment bookings, showroom operating hours, map/location details, and direct contact pathways.
