# Do Not Copy Restrictions

To maintain high reusability and unique brand positioning, the following reference attributes from the BoConcept direction were intentionally avoided:

- **Branding**: Logos, specific tagline, and proprietary typography.
- **Visual Signatures**: Direct copy of exact product collections, furniture dimensions, and specific layout combinations.
- **Imagery & Copy**: Identical product descriptions, collection stories, or images.
- **Direct Global Focus**: Purely international e-commerce patterns were modified to support practical, localized Turkish-market flows (WhatsApp, local address framing, showroom visit booking).
