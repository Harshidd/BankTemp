# Template Summary: Authority Expert Brand

**Template Name:** Authority Expert Brand
**Category:** Personal Brand
**Status:** Ready / Multi-Page System

## Concept
A premium, calm, and authority-focused website system for experts, consultants, and solo-founders. Unlike SaaS templates which focus on "product features", this template focuses on "expertise-to-outcome" storytelling.

## Target Audience
- High-ticket consultants (Operations, Strategy, Design)
- Digital educators & Course creators
- Executive coaches & Advisors
- Solo entrepreneurs building niche businesses

## Visual DNA
- **Colors:** Warm stones, deep charcols, and sophisticated ambers (amber-500).
- **Typography:** Serif headings for authority, clean sans-serif bodies for clarity.
- **Vibe:** "I have been doing this for 10 years and I know the shortcut."

## Key Conversion Points
- **Newsletter Capture:** Prominent but tasteful email opt-ins.
- **Offer Tiering:** Clear path from free content to high-ticket consulting.
- **Social Proof:** Outcome-driven testimonials and "seen in" credibility strips.
