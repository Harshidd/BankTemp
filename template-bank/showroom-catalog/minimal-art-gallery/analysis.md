# Reference Analysis: Esther Schipper + David Zwirner

## Borrowed Concepts
- **Homepage Restraint:** Clean, large hero image with zero noise.
- **Museum-Grade Spacing:** Generous whitespace around components, ensuring visual calmness.
- **Neutral Palette:** Neutral backgrounds and dark text let artwork colors pop without competition.
- **Alphabetical/Clean Roster:** High predictability in artist and exhibition lists.

## Exclusions
- **Unnecessary E-Commerce Chrome:** Zero online purchasing flows. This is a lead-generation and cultural-awareness template.
- **High Animation Density:** No spring or bounce transforms. Transitions use slow, gradual fades.
