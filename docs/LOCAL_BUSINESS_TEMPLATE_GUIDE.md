# LOCAL_BUSINESS_TEMPLATE_GUIDE.md

For small local businesses, SEO and high-intent structure are more important than complex animations or unique branding. This guide defines what makes a strong, reusable local business starter.

## 📍 Purpose
The primary goal of a local business template is **conversion** (phone calls, bookings, visits) and **local SEO relevance** (services-plus-location keywords).

## 🏙️ Core Section Hierarchy

Every local business template MUST include these sections:

1. **Hero (Clear Service + Location):**
    - High-impact visual + Clear `H1` (Service + City).
    - Immediate Call-to-Action (Call Now / Get Quote).
2. **Trust Bar (Social Proof):**
    - Google Ratings, Checkatrade, Yelp, or industry certifications.
3. **Core Services Grid:**
    - Icon + Title + Brief description of what they do.
    - Each service should be ready to link to its own sub-page.
4. **Local Authority / Experience:**
    - "Why Choose Us?" - Highlighting local expertise and years in business.
5. **Process / How It Works:**
    - 3-4 steps (Book → Visit → Success) to reduce friction.
6. **Social Proof (Testimonials):**
    - Real-world reviews from the local area.
7. **Maps & Contact Integration:**
    - Embedded Google Maps (placeholder).
    - Physical address, phone number, and opening hours.
8. **Service Areas List:**
    - List of towns/regions served (crucial for local SEO).
9. **Conversion Footer:**
    - Final CTA and easy-to-find contact info.

## 📐 SEO-First Structure Guidelines

- **Semantic HTML:** Use `<h1>` to `<h6>` in a logical, hierarchical order.
- **Service-Area Focus:** Add a data-driven "Service Area" component for easy list updates.
- **Contact Info in Footer:** NAP (Name, Address, Phone) must be consistently present on every page.
- **Fast Loading:** Minimize heavy animations and optimize images.
- **Google Maps Ready:** Use a generic Google Maps iframe or Leaflet JS placeholder.

## 🛠️ Reusable Assets for Local Business
- **Standard Service Icons:** Plumbing, Electric, Law, Medical, etc.
- **Map Placeholders:** Clean, neutral map styling.
- **Trust Badges:** Generic "Verified" or "Highly Rated" badges.

## 📋 Evaluation: Is this a Good Local Business Template?
- [ ] Is the primary phone number/contact button visible on load?
- [ ] Is the service list clearly scannable?
- [ ] Are the location keywords (City/Region) easily updated via strings?
- [ ] Is the site crawlable by search engine bots (clean HTML)?
- [ ] Does it establish immediate "local trust"?
