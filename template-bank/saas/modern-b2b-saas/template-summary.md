# Template: Modern B2B SaaS (Multi-Page Expansion)

**Category:** SaaS
**Status:** READY (V2 - Multi-Page)
**MRKDEV ID:** mrkdev-saas-modern-b2b-v2

## Purpose
A premium, high-velocity website starter designed for modern B2B SaaS products that demand structural clarity and sub-millisecond execution patterns. This template has been expanded from a single landing page into a full 6-page professional product existence.

## Target Use Cases
- **Development Tools:** IDEs, CI/CD, Git interfaces, performance monitoring.
- **Workflow Platforms:** Project management, task orchestration, collaborative whiteboards.
- **Data & Ops:** Observability, infrastructure management, governance toolkits.
- **High-Performance SaaS:** Any product where speed, architectural depth, and premium minimalism are core value propositions.

## Multi-Page Architecture
1. **Home:** The structural hub focused on impact hero, trust signals, and high-level feature discovery.
2. **Features:** A deep architectural exploration of product capabilities with categorized deep-dives.
3. **Pricing:** Clear, tiered packages (Starter, Premium, Enterprise) with feature comparisons and objection-diffusing FAQs.
4. **About Us:** Narrative-driven philosophy on structural clarity, company values, and team scaling metrics.
5. **Contact (Inquiry Hub):** Sales-friendly portal with direct communication channels and professional demo-booking forms.
6. **FAQ:** A structured help hub addressing architectural, billing, and security queries for high-velocity teams.

## Reusable Structural Strengths
- **Linear-Inspired Minimalist Aesthetic:** Focuses on typography and structural hierarchy over flashy gradients.
- **State-Based Previews:** Unified in-browser navigation for a realistic browsing experience within the catalog.
- **High-Density Information Layouts:** Designed to handle complex feature storytelling without clutter.
