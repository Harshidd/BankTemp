# Customization Notes

How to customize and reuse the template for new products and showrooms:

## Branding & Colors
- Swap out the text logo in `Navbar.tsx` and `Footer.tsx` with your custom SVG or image.
- Modify standard Tailwind colors within the `pages/` directory to suit any premium palette (e.g. replacing neutral cremes with rich grays or dark modes).

## Catalog Items
- Update the mock products array inside `pages/Catalog.tsx` to display true showroom items.
- Customize specific features like pricing tags, available families, or inquiry channels.

## Local Adaptations
- Easily update address, opening hours, and phone numbers in `pages/Contact.tsx`.
- Adjust messaging to match local market contexts.
