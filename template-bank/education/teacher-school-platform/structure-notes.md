# Structure Notes - Teacher School Platform

## Information Architecture
1. **Home:** High-impact institutional hero, value proposition, and trust blocks.
2. **Solutions:** Detailed breakdown of platform capabilities for different education segments.
3. **Teachers / Schools:** Dual-audience page explaining specific benefits for individual educators and institutional admins.
4. **Reporting:** Dedicated showcase for learning analytics and progress tracking systems.
5. **Pricing:** Education-focused plan comparison and "Request Quote/Demo" pathways.
6. **Contact / Demo:** Frictionless inquiry page for school partnerships.
7. **FAQ:** Structured resource for common onboarding and administrative questions.

## Design DNA
- **Typography:** Rounded sans-serif for clarity and approachable authority.
- **Colors:** Deep Institutional Blue (Trust), Vibrant Growth Green (Progress), Neutral Slate (Foundation).
- **Cards:** Soft shadowed cards with 24px radius to match modern education software standards.
- **Icons:** Minimalist geometric iconography representing tools, data, and community.
