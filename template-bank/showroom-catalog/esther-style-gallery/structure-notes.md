# Structure Notes: Esther Style Gallery

## 1. Typography & Aesthetic
- **Palette:** Warm, sophisticated `zinc-50` background contrasted with sharp `zinc-950` headings and rich cinematic photography.
- **Typography:** Direct, lightweight uppercase sans-serif (`font-sans`) providing an authoritative gallery presence.

## 2. Page Breakdown
- **Home:** Massive featured visual display with direct links to current exhibitions, featured artists, and editorial news.
- **Artists:** Clean left/right staggered profiles featuring immersive portfolio blocks.
- **Exhibitions:** Visual storytelling of current and upcoming programming across global locations.
- **Press & News:** Editorial chronology of institutional achievements.
- **About:** Gallery mission and history paired with strong architectural visuals.
- **Locations & Contact:** Easy visitor guidance across global spaces.
