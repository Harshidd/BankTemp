# Template: High-Trust Local Service (Multi-Page Expansion)

**Category:** Local Business
**Status:** READY (V2 - Multi-Page)
**MRKDEV ID:** mrkdev-local-high-trust-v2

## Purpose
A comprehensive, conversion-focused website template designed for local service businesses that rely on trust, transparency, and rapid local response. This template has been expanded from a single landing page into a full 6-page professional business website.

## Target Use Cases
- **Home Services:** Plumbing, HVAC, Electrical, Pest Control, Painting.
- **Maintenance:** Cleaning services, appliance repair, property management support.
- **Contractors:** General builders, pool maintenance, landscaping.
- **Consulting:** Local clinics, advisory services, or any professional service with a regional coverage focus.

## Multi-Page Architecture
1. **Home:** Conversion-centric hub with lead capture, service preview, and credibility signals.
2. **Services:** Detailed breakdown of core offerings, price transparency, and specialty categories.
3. **Areas:** SEO-optimized service area directory with regional coverage focus and map-ready visual.
4. **About:** Trust-building company origin, core values, and professional technician/team previews.
5. **Reviews:** Centralized proof listings with star-ratings, verified client metrics, and before/after proof patterns.
6. **Contact:** Frictionless inquiry hub with multi-channel contact support (Phone, WhatsApp, Form), location NAP details, and emergency priority logic.

## Reusable Structural Strengths
- **Trust Strip:** Instant authority through industry badge placeholders.
- **Process Guide:** Lowering conversion friction with a clear 1-2-3 execution strategy.
- **Local Directory:** Grid-based SEO coverage listings easy to localize to any region.
- **Emergency Focus:** Integrated urgency patterns for high-intent repair calls.
