# Analysis: ai-feature-promo

## Reference Site Analysis
**Primary Reference**: [Notion Mail](https://www.notion.com/product/mail)

### Extracted Patterns
1. **Vertical Rhythm**: A series of breathy, focused sections that each tell one part of the story.
2. **Feature Grouping**: Showing how small details add up to a major workflow change.
3. **Momentum Display**: Using release notes to signal that the product is alive and evolving.
4. **Acquisition Path**: Direct, simple CTA (Join Waitlist or Get Started).

### Intentional Exclusions
- Notion's specific "minimalist list" UI.
- The heavy reliance on internal Notion-specific branding.
- The sidebar-heavy product interface (this template stays brand-blind).

## Design DNA: AI Feature Promo
Compared to a standard App Launch Promo, this template is:
- **Calmer**: Less aggressive "Hacker" aesthetic, more "Productivity Editorial".
- **Lighter**: More whitespace, thinner borders, softer shadows.
- **Story-First**: Focuses on *how* your life changes, not just *what* the buttons do.

## Competitive Positioning
This template sits between "Basic Landing Page" and "Full SaaS Dashboard". It is the perfect home for a standalone feature or a boutique AI tool that values design excellence.
