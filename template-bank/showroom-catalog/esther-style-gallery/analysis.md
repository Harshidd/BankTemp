# Reference Analysis: Esther Schipper

## Borrowed Logic
- **Immersive Visual Weight:** Cinematic, widescreen image presence as the primary driver on the Home and Exhibitions pages.
- **Restrained Typography:** Pure uppercase, spaced-out sans-serif headers denoting serious artistic curatorial intent.
- **Wide Spaces:** Slower horizontal rhythm, creating a distinct physical sense of traversing a curated gallery.

## What Was Intentionally Excluded
- **Complicated WebGL Scroll-Effects:** Replaced by smooth, performant standard Tailwind transitions to maintain high loading speeds and direct adaptability.
