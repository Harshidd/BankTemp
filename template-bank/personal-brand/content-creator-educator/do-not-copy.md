# DO NOT COPY

This template is inspired by high-performing creator-educator sites (like Ali Abdaal's and others in the space). However, it is fundamentally a **Brand-Agnostic Starter Template**.

## Absolute Prohibitions

1. **No Exact Copying of Content:**
   - Do NOT use exact phrases like "Join the Part-Time YouTuber Academy".
   - Do NOT use exact slogans, headlines, or value propositions belonging to Ali Abdaal or any specific creator.

2. **No Signature Branding:**
   - Do NOT use specific custom "Ali Abdaal" signature fonts or handwriting SVG assets unless the end-user provides their own.
   - Do NOT copy specific proprietary icon sets or mascots.

3. **No Exact Color Copying:**
   - Our default color palette uses vibrant emerald/amber/slate to maintain a distinct "MRKDEV Template" identity. Do NOT perfectly mirror the exact hex codes of the reference site unless explicitly requested by the user during the customization phase.

4. **No Structural Tributes:**
   - The goal is not to perfectly recreate Ali Abdaal's website structure pixel-by-pixel. We abstracted the *principles* of his site (content-first, clear courses, strong newsletter integration, energetic vibe). This template must stand on its own as an original structure.

5. **No Blind Replication of Offers:**
   - Do not copy exact course names, module breakdowns, or community tiers structure if they are unique intellectual property of the referenced creator.

## Remember
Your job is to provide the *engine* (the code, the components, the layout strategy). The *fuel* (the content, the specific branding) must come from the user.
