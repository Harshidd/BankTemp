# Structure Notes: Modern B2B SaaS (V2 Multi-Page)

## Design Hierarchy
- **Header:** Sticky Navbar with state-driven navigation (Home, Features, Pricing, About, Contact, FAQ). Includes a subtle **MRKDEV Template** identity badge.
- **Background Accents:** Unified glass-morphism background logic with fixed blue-emerald blur points to maintain depth consistency across all pages.

## Page Breakdown & Logic
### 1. Home (Structural Hub)
- **Hero:** Linear-inspired, high-impact headline with a focus on speed and clarity.
- **Trust Marquee:** Minimalist logo strip with grayscale scaling.
- **Feature Discovery Grid:** High-level entry point to deeper architectural features.
- **Value Storytelling:** High-density feature cards with detailed highlights.

### 2. Features (Deep Architecture)
- **Problem-to-Insight Sections:** Categorized deep-dives (Clarity, Velocity, Governance, Connectivity).
- **Bullet-Level Precision:** Specific feature-point grids (Offline-first, RBAC, Webhooks).
- **Integration Strip:** Grayscale cluster of popular ecosystem partnerships.

### 3. Pricing (Tiered Scaling)
- **Grid-based Tiers:** 3-column pricing system (Starter, Premium, Enterprise).
- **Objection FAQ:** Small summary list to address billing/portability concerns immediately.
- **Comparison Logic:** Minimalist horizontal comparison strip for core performance features.

### 4. About Us (Narrative / Team)
- **Philosophy Hero:** Philosophy-driven hero focused on "fragmentation of clarity."
- **Values Grid:** Premium minimalist cards with iconography (Target, Heart, Shield, Layers).
- **Performance Metrics:** Trust-building stats (Global Relationships, Core Integrity).
- **Department Placeholders:** Grid of "Built by builders" department-level SVGs.

### 5. Contact (Sales Inquiry Hub)
- **Inquiry Split:** Dual column layout (Direct Support Channels vs. Premium Demo Form).
- **Demo Booking Logic:** Categorized "Inquiry Purpose" dropdown for pre-sales qualification.
- **Trust Signals Strip:** Mono-styled compliance badges (SOC2, GDPR, Uptime).

### 6. FAQ (Knowledge Hub)
- **Category Navigation Strip:** Horizontal nav for deeper research (Core, Billing, Security, Ops).
- **Q&A Blocks:** Large, readable Q&A cards with bold headings and high-density answers.
- **Docs Hub Transition:** Secondary CTA for "Knowledge Hub" and "Chat Support."

## Footer (Site Directory)
- **5-Column Layout:** Product, Company, Resources, Branding, and MRKDEV Identity Signature.
- **Platform Status:** Sub-indicator for "Structure Stable" to create architectural trust.
