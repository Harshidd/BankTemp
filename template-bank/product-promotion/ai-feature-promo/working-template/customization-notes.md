# Customization Notes: ai-feature-promo

## Branding & Identity
- **Colors**: Change the primary accent in `globals.css` or Tailwind config. The template defaults to a calm blue/slate mix.
- **Typography**: Optimized for sans-serif fonts (Inter/Outfit). Easy to swap in `layout.tsx`.
- **Logos**: Replace the `BrandLogo` component in `Navbar` and `Footer`.

## Content Adaptation
- **Product Name**: Global find & replace for "AI Feature" placeholder.
- **Features**: Edit `Features.tsx` to group capabilities into 2-3 main pillars.
- **Workflows**: Designed to be generic; swap icons and titles to match specific AI use cases (e.g., Coding, Writing, Analysis).
- **Pricing**: The current model assumes a simple Personal/Team split. Can be expanded to multi-tier easily.

## Turkey-Local Readiness
- Headings are designed with extra line-height and flexible containers to handle longer Turkish translations.
- Contact forms and FAQ sections use standard layouts that are common in the Turkish SaaS market.

## Pro Tips
- Use high-quality dashboard mockups or clean SVG illustrations in the Hero area.
- Keep descriptions short; the design relies on whitespace for its premium feel.
