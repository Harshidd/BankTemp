# DO NOT COPY: Modern Interactive Learning

## Branding & Visual Identity
- **Brilliant.org Identity:** Do not copy the specific green/blue illustrations, the character-based coaching visuals, the "Guided Path" specific UI signatures, or the "15 Minute Daily" messaging.
- **Wording:** Do not use Brilliant's specific course titles, "Interactive Stories" terminology, or original course descriptions.
- **Logos:** No Brilliant logos or specific icon styles (e.g. their custom 3D scientific icons).

## UX/UI Signatures
- **Question Logic:** Do not copy the specific layout of Brilliant's interactive multiple-choice or drag-and-drop questions.
- **Feedback:** Do not copy the "Correct/Incorrect" specific sound-bites or visual pop-up signatures.
- **Gamification:** Avoid copying their specific "Streak" visual logic or "XP" level bars.

## Structural Exclusions (Intentional)
- **Personalized Path AI:** This template provides a visual mockup of adaptive learning but does not include (or require for the preview) a real AI recommendation engine.
- **Subject Depth:** This is a website template for an academy platform, not a full LMS core (it describes the subjects, but does not house thousands of interactive lessons). It focus on "Pre-Sales/Enrollment" conversion flow.
- **Social Learning:** Community feed mockups were excluded to prioritize the individual "Mastery Loop" value proposition.
