# Structure Notes: High-Trust Local Business (V2 Multi-Page)

## Design Hierarchy
- **Header:** Sticky Navbar with "Call Now" emergency priority button and dynamic navigation links (Home, Services, Areas, About, Reviews, Contact). Includes a subtle **MRKDEV Template** identity badge.
- **Top-Bar:** Low-friction urgency band (Available 24/7, Local Coverage Area).

## Page Breakdown & Logic
### 1. Home (Landing Hub)
- **Hero:** Leads with immediate [Service] in [City] H1 for local SEO relevance. Integrates a rapid lead capture form.
- **Trust Strip:** Instant credibility via industry-standard certification placeholders.
- **Why Choose Us:** Benefit-led value blocks (Licensed, Background Checked, 24/7 Response).
- **Service Highlights Grid:** High-level access point to the full Services page. 

### 2. Services (Decision Phase)
- **Categorized Detail Blocks:** 4-column-friendly layout (Maintenance, Emergency, Safety, Major Projects).
- **Transparency Strip:** Explains pricing models (Flat Rate vs. Estimates) to lower barriers to entry.
- **Contextual CTAs:** Encourages individual quote requests for specific service types.

### 3. Areas (SEO Focus)
- **Service Area Directory Grid:** 5-column city/area listing placeholder for regional coverage.
- **Map Mockup:** High-trust visual placeholder for HQ location identification.
- **Distance Commitments:** Integrated logic for "30-60 Minute Response" messaging.

### 4. About (Story / Trust)
- **Founding Narrative:** Simple, humanized 1-2-3 company evolution storyline.
- **Core Value Icons:** Transparent accountability, expert vetting, and client-first metrics.
- **Technician Placeholders:** Visual representation grid for verified professionals.

### 5. Reviews (Social Proof)
- **Proof Grid:** 3-column star-rating cards with verified buyer tags and localized location tags.
- **Evidence Strip:** Placeholder for before/after visual proof patterns. 

### 6. Contact (Conversion Hub)
- **Split Hero/Form:** Focuses on immediate inquiry with dedicated fields for Service Category.
- **NAP Consistency Row:** Detailed Name, Address, Phone (NAP) placeholders for business identity/local SEO.
- **Emergency Priority CTA:** High-contrast blue-white urgency block for rapid dial-out.

## Footer (Site Directory)
- **4-Column Layout:** Navigation links, Local coverage links, Business Hours table, and a dedicated "Need Service?" conversion call.
- **Branding:** Discrete **MRKDEV STARTER TEMPLATE** signature at the base.
