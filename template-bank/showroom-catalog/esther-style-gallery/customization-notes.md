# Customization Notes

## Immersive Imagery
- To retain the reference's atmospheric tone, it is vital to source high-end, professionally composed exhibition and artwork photography.
- The hero image block uses an `aspect-[16/9]`, while the artist and secondary exhibition lists use `aspect-[4/3]` and `aspect-[4/5]` profiles.

## Language Translation
- Headings are written concisely in uppercase tracking (`tracking-widest`), making them highly tolerant of longer localized strings upon future Turkish translation.
