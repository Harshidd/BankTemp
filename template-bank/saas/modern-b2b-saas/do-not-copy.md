# Do Not Copy: Modern B2B SaaS (V2 Multi-Page)

The following components and styles were extracted from **Linear** and **Figma** for structural product logic. They have been generalized into this **MRKDEV Modern B2B SaaS Template** and must NOT be used to recreate the original branding of the reference sites.

## 1. Do Not Copy Original Branding
- **Business Names:** Do not use "Linear", "Figma", or "Linear-Lite" branding.
- **Logos:** The original logos for these businesses must not be used. This template uses a generic "S" (Starter) logo placeholder.
- **Color Palettes:** The exact primary/secondary hex codes from the original sites have been tweaked to a more neutral blue-emerald palette. Do not revert to the original site hex values.

## 2. Do Not Copy Proprietary Messaging
- **Unique Taglines:** Do not use proprietary slogans like "The issue tracker you'll actually love" (Linear) or "How you design, align, and build" (Figma).
- **Service Claims:** Do not copy specific local awards or original year-of-founding claims unless they are true for the new customer.
- **Testimonials:** All reviews in this template are [Client Name] placeholders. Never use actual named reviews from Linear or Figma.

## 3. Do Not Copy Personal/Business Images
- **Product Screenshots:** No original screenshots of the reference products have been used. This template uses generic SVG placeholders for product mockups.
- **Facility Photos:** Do not use any real photos of the original businesses' offices or fleet vehicles. 

## 4. Specific Structural Removals
- **Original Dashboard UI:** If adding a real dashboard, use a fresh design system. Do not attempt to replicate the exact custom border-radii or gradient styles from the original references.

## 5. Transition Rule
This is a **MRKDEV Started Template**. Use its structural foundation only. Every branding-specific text, image, and color must be customized for the final SaaS client.
