# Analysis & Extraction

## Extracted Structure
- **Value Separation:** Separated user experience paths for individual teachers and whole schools/districts.
- **Progress Visualization Concept:** The idea of showing "Data-at-a-glance" in a public-facing page to sell the SaaS value.
- **Tiered Onboarding:** Structural logic of "Sign Up Free" -> "Contact for Schools".
- **Three-Column Trust:** Testimonials and trust badges using the 3-column rhythm observed in high-performing education sites.

## Innovation / Differences
- **Institutional Scale:** This template is slightly more "Admin" focused than the reference, making it more suitable for B2B Education SaaS.
- **Modular Dashboard Preview:** Added a specific section for showcasing how a product's dashboard looks, which is less prominent in the reference.
- **Direct Demo Path:** Unified "Request Demo" flow integrated into multiple pages for higher conversion potential.
