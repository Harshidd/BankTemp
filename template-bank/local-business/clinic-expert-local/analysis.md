# Analysis: Clinic Expert Local

## Reference Breakdown (Structural)
The reference site `clarendonvision.com` was analyzed for its effectiveness in high-trust local lead generation. The following structural elements were extracted:

1. **Information Hierarchy**: Placing specific, named expert trust (Doctors/Specialists) directly on the homepage.
2. **Specialized Grouping**: Breaking services down into "Symptoms" or "Core Areas" rather than just a long list.
3. **The Multi-Page Flow**: Using a full 7-page structure to build authority. Search engines and high-intent patients value detailed About/FAQ/Team pages for specialists.
4. **Appointment Prominence**: Making "Book Appointment" a distinct, colorful constant in the navigation.

## Design Decisions
- **Typography**: Using Inter (San-serif) with Heavy/Black weights for headings to create an "Editorial/Scientific" feel.
- **Color Psychology**:
  - **Navy**: Authority and stability.
  - **Indigo**: Intelligence and modernity.
  - **Slate/White**: Cleanliness and transparency.
- **Micro-interactions**: Subtle `animate-in` effects and hover-scaling on cards to make the experience feel "alive" and responsive.
- **Separation**: Distinguished from the existing "Handyman/Home Repair" style by using more photography-led layouts and clinical data points over "Review Stars" alone.
