# Interior Studio Showroom

## Overview
A project-led, calm, and elegant interior studio website template. Built to balance high-fidelity project showcases with material library exploration and practical service consultation flows.

## Core Features
- **Project-Led Structure:** Projects are the primary visual drivers, supported by clean masonry-style grids.
- **Material Discovery:** A dedicated Collections page designed for material, fabric, and texture exploration without feeling like a harsh retail catalog.
- **Phased Service Explanation:** Services are broken down clearly from discovery to execution.
- **Showroom Visit Flow:** The Contact page is framed around visiting the showroom and scheduling consultations, not just a generic form.
- **Calm Architecture:** Slower transition durations, zero hyperactive scale effects, and a restrained color palette (stone-50 to stone-900).

## Why This Exists
Standard agency and SaaS templates are too loud, bouncy, and abstract for high-end interior studios. This template strips away the "startup theater" and relies on strong imagery, clean typography, and a grounded, trustworthy rhythm inspired by premium European furniture and interior brands (e.g., BoConcept).
