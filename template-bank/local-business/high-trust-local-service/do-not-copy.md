# Do Not Copy: High-Trust Local Business (V2 Multi-Page)

The following components and styles were extracted from **MyPlumbingPros** and **SunsetHC** for structural conversion logic. They have been generalized into this **MRKDEV Local Service Template** and must NOT be used to recreate the original branding of the reference sites.

## 1. Do Not Copy Original Branding
- **Business Names:** Do not use "MyPlumbingPros", "My Electrical Pros", or "Sunset Heating & Cooling" branding.
- **Logos:** The original logos for these businesses must not be used. This template uses a generic "L" (Local) logo placeholder.
- **Color Palettes:** The exact primary/secondary hex codes from the original sites have been tweaked. Do not revert to the original site hex values.

## 2. Do Not Copy Proprietary Messaging
- **Unique Taglines:** Do not use proprietary slogans like "The Pros who Care" or specific SunsetHC taglines.
- **Service Claims:** Do not copy specific local awards or original year-of-founding claims unless they are true for the new customer.
- **Testimonials:** All reviews in this template are [Client Name] placeholders. Never use actual named reviews from MyPlumbingPros or SunsetHC.

## 3. Do Not Copy Personal/Business Images
- **Technician Photos:** No original photos of personnel from the reference sites have been used. This template uses generic SVG placeholders.
- **Facility Photos:** Do not use any real photos of the original businesses' offices or fleet vehicles. 

## 4. Specific Structural Removals
- **Original Map Layers:** If adding a real map, use a fresh API key. Do not attempt to replicate the exact custom marker styles from the original references.
- **Original Form Logic:** The inquiry form in this template is a generic React form. Do not copy the specific CRM-integrated form fields from the original sites.

## 5. Transition Rule
This is a **MRKDEV Started Template**. Use its structural foundation only. Every branding-specific text, image, and color must be customized for the final local business client.
