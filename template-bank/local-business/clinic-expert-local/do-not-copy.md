# Do Not Copy: Clinic Expert Local

## Reference Integrity
While inspired by high-quality clinical practices like `clarendonvision.com`, this template is a structural evolution, not a verbatim clone.

**Do NOT include the following from the reference site in final branded builds:**
- **Branding**: Specific logos, font pairings, or exact hex colors from Clarendon Vision.
- **Provider Names**: Do not use real names of the clinicians from the reference site.
- **Patient Claims**: Do not copy specific patient results or case study wording.
- **Visual Signatures**: Avoid replicating the exact overlapping shape arrangements used by the reference if they are trademarked or unique to their brand identity.
- **Bylines**: Do not use "Vision" or "Eye" specific mission statements unless building an eye clinic. 

**This template is a generic "Specialist Clinic" shell.** All content provided is placeholder-level "Medical Latin" style text.
