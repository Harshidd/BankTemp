# Structure Notes: Minimal Art Gallery

## 1. Typography & Colors
- **Palette:** Relying on the `neutral` scale (`neutral-50` background, `neutral-900` text) to emulate a clean, well-lit white cube gallery environment.
- **Typography:** Contrast between lightweight sans-serif for structured navigation/metadata and a crisp italicized serif for artwork titles.

## 2. Navigation Flow
- Fully operational pages: Home, Exhibitions, Works, Artists, About, Visit & Contact.
- Includes standard `MinimalBackLink` for easy preview backtracking.
