# Do Not Copy - Structural Analysis

The following elements were **intentionally excluded** from this template to ensure it remains a reusable starter rather than a clone:

- **Branding Specifics:** Avoided all Duolingo colors (Exact green/orange hex codes), mascots (Owl motif), and specific wording ("Gems", "Streaks").
- **Gamification Mechanics:** Excluded product-specific progress bars/streak counters to keep the template broadly applicable to any institutional software.
- **Content Blocks:** Removed Duolingo-specific training modules ("Professional Development Course") in favor of generic "Teacher Resources" logic.
- **Graphic Art:** All decorative elements are geometric or abstract placeholders, not derived from the reference's character art.
